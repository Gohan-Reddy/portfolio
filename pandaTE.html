    Introduction

    Implemented a comprehensive Business Management System (BMS) using Laravel and JavaScript, enabling users to efficiently track their business activities. The system encompasses a wide range of functionalities, including product and customer management, invoice and estimate generation, as well as cash flow tracking. Employed dynamic frontend calculations using JavaScript frameworks such as Sahi and DOM manipulation techniques, coupled with event listeners to ensure smooth user experience and alleviate server load. Contributed to enhancing scalability and performance while ensuring seamless functionality across the application.
